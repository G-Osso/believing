About us!

Pictures

This is our School of languages. It was built in 2010. The school has offered access to a wide range of academic opportunities. Our courses are designed to teach and motivate students of all ages. The staff is dedicated to providing them with the best experience they can have.

We are a private school of languages founded in 2009. It is located in Lomas's centre. Since 2020, it is attached to AEXALEVI, where students sit for the International Exams(externally or internally). We have been working online since 2017. Teachers are trained to deliver enganging classroom classes as well as online classes. Our aim at this school has always been opening students minds to seek for opportunities, getting students involed in an interesting way where they can express freely and fluently. Students have the possibility of enrolling in Phonetics clasess as well as conversation lessons. Each class will be held with technological devices where students get to work alone, in pais or in groups. All the skills will take place in an organized way taking into account authentic material and the regular books.

Until now, we are teaching English and Portuguese. This is subject to change.
